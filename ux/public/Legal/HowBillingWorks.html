How Billing Works

A plain-English guide to payments and your account balance on LeisurePlan.app

LeisurePlan.app uses a simple prepaid balance system. You add funds when you need them, and pay only for what you use. There are no subscriptions, no monthly fees, and your balance never expires.

The Basics

Running a retirement simulation requires computing power. Each time you submit a question to the AI planning assistant, the system processes your request and returns charts, projections, and a summary. That processing has a small cost — typically a few cents per query — which is drawn from your prepaid balance.

Think of it like a prepaid phone card: you top up your account with real dollars, and those dollars are spent as you use the service. Simple follow-up questions tend to cost less than complex multi-part analyses.

No surprises. Before each query runs, we show you the estimated cost. If your balance is too low to cover it, we'll let you know and prompt you to add funds — your query won't run until you do.

Adding Funds

You can add funds to your account at any time from the Billing page (accessible from the navigation menu). Choose from one of the available top-up amounts:

  • $10 — a good starting point for new users
  • $25 — suitable for regular use
  • $50 — ideal if you plan to run many scenarios or share the tool with a spouse or advisor

Click your chosen amount and you'll be taken to a secure Stripe checkout page to complete payment by credit or debit card. Once your payment is confirmed, your balance updates immediately and you can continue running queries right away.

Example

You add $25 to your account. Over the following weeks you run 40 queries, each costing an average of $0.45. That uses $18.00, leaving you with $7.00 remaining.

How Costs Are Calculated

Each query is priced based on the amount of text processed — both what you send to the AI and what it generates in response. This is measured in tokens (roughly, groups of a few characters each). Simple questions with short answers cost less; detailed analyses with long, structured responses cost more.

We calculate the cost automatically — you never need to think about tokens directly. The estimated cost is shown on screen before your query runs, and the exact cost is shown in your balance after it completes.

What Happens When Your Balance Is Low

Before each query, we check whether your current balance covers the estimated cost. Here's what happens in each scenario:

  • Balance is sufficient: Your query runs normally. Your balance is updated after the results are returned.
  • Balance is insufficient: Your query is paused. You'll see a notification showing your current balance and the estimated cost of the query, with a link to the Billing page to top up. Once you've added funds, you can re-submit your question.

Your balance is always shown in the top corner of the screen so you can keep track without visiting the Billing page.

Refunds

Your unused balance belongs to you. If you'd like a refund of any remaining funds — for any reason — just email us at support@leisureplan.app and we'll process it back to your original payment method within five business days.

Only unused funds can be refunded. Amounts already consumed by completed queries are not refundable.

If you plan to close your account and have a remaining balance, please request your refund before you close the account so we can process it smoothly.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does my balance expire?

No. Your balance has no expiry date. Funds you add today will still be there in a year.

Is my payment information stored on your servers?

No. All payment processing is handled by Stripe, one of the world's leading payment platforms. We never see or store your card details. Only your account balance is stored on our end.

Can I get a receipt for my top-up?

Yes. Stripe will email you a receipt automatically after each successful payment.

What if my query fails or returns an error?

If a query fails due to a technical error on our end, no cost will be deducted from your balance. If you believe you were charged for a failed query, contact us and we'll investigate and credit you if appropriate.

Can I see a history of what I've been charged?

Your balance is updated after every query and shown in the top bar. A full query history with per-query costs is available on the Billing page.

Will the cost per query ever change?

We may adjust rates in the future, but we will always notify you in advance. Any funds already in your account at the time of a rate change are not affected — they retain their dollar value.

What currency are charges in?

All charges are in U.S. dollars (USD).

Questions?

If you have any questions about billing or your account, we're happy to help.
