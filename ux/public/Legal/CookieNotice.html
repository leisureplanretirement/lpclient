Cookie Notice

This Cookie Notice explains how Leisure Plan Retirement Inc ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit LeisurePlan.app. It should be read alongside our Privacy Notice.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They allow the site to remember information about your visit — such as whether you are logged in — so the site can function correctly and you do not have to re-authenticate on every page.

We also use local storage, a similar browser-based mechanism, to retain session state within the application.

2. Cookies We Use

We use only strictly necessary cookies. We do not use advertising, analytics, or tracking cookies, and we do not permit third-party advertisers to place cookies on our site.

Cookie / Storage Key Provider Purpose Duration
auth0.* Auth0 Maintains your authenticated session so you remain logged in while using the application. Session / up to 7 days
__stripe_*, __cfruid Stripe Used during the payment checkout process to handle your payment securely and prevent fraud. Only active during a checkout session. Session
Application local storage LeisurePlan.app Stores your current session identifier and UI preferences in your browser so the application can restore your last state on return visits. Until you clear browser data or log out

Because these cookies are strictly necessary for the service to function, they cannot be disabled without preventing you from logging in or using the application.

3. What We Do Not Use

  • Advertising or targeting cookies — we do not serve or track advertisements.
  • Third-party analytics cookies — we do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or similar tracking services.
  • Social media tracking pixels — we do not embed social media widgets that track you across sites.

4. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to view, block, and delete cookies through their settings. Common browser help pages:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions

Please note that blocking or deleting the authentication cookies listed in Section 2 will prevent you from logging in to LeisurePlan.app.
